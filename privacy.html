Privacy Policy

Effective: 20 Sep 2025

Ironwell (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a fitness logging app. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data We Collect

  • Account: email, optional first name.
  • Workout data: exercises, sets, reps, weights, sessions, PRs.
  • Device/technical: IP, timestamps, and basic diagnostics (to operate and secure the service).

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve the app (core functionality, sync, analytics internal to our product).
  • Account support, security, and to communicate important changes.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal data. We share only with service providers (e.g., hosting/database) under contract to operate the app, or when required by law.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account in-app: Profile → Delete Account. Deleting removes the account and associated data from our records unless we must retain certain data by law (e.g., fraud prevention). See also our Support page.

Your Choices

  • Access/Update: manage profile in-app.
  • Delete: in-app via Profile → Delete Account.

Children

Not for children under the minimum age in your region (generally 16+).

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect data; no method is 100% secure.

Contact

Email: support@yourdomain.com

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

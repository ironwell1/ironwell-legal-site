Privacy Policy

Effective: 20 Sep 2025

Ironwell ("we", "our", "us") provides a fitness logging app. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data We Collect

  • Account: email, optional first name.
  • Workout data: exercises, sets, reps, weights, sessions, personal records.
  • Technical: IP address, timestamps, device/app diagnostics (to operate and secure the service).

How We Use Data

  • Provide core functionality (auth, sync, workout logging).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug issues.
  • Improve the app (aggregate analytics internal to our product).
  • Communicate important updates about the service.

Sharing

We don't sell personal data. We share data with service providers (e.g., hosting/database) under contract to operate the app, and when required by law. Providers must handle data appropriately and only for our purposes.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account in-app: Profile ? Delete Account. Deleting removes your account and associated data from our active systems, except where we must retain limited information as required by law (e.g., fraud prevention, legal obligations). Backups roll off on a schedule.

Your Choices

  • Access/Update: manage your profile in-app.
  • Delete: use Profile ? Delete Account to remove your account and data.
  • Questions: contact us at the addresses below.

Children

The app is not intended for children under the minimum age in your region (generally 16+). Do not use the app if you are under the applicable age threshold.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

International

Data may be processed and stored in the United States or other locations where our providers operate. Your use of the app means you understand your data may be transferred to these locations.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

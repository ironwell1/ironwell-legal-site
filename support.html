Support

Help, troubleshooting, and how to contact us.

Important Note

Ironwell provides general fitness logging only. We cannot provide medical advice, diagnose injuries, or recommend treatments. If you have pain, dizziness, chest discomfort, or any medical concerns, please contact a qualified healthcare professional.

Delete Your Account

You can delete your Ironwell account at any time in the app:

Profile → Delete Account

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile and workout data from our active systems.

Having an Issue?

Please email support@ironwellapp.com with the details below so we can resolve the issue quickly.

  • A clear subject line, e.g., Bug: crash when adding new exercise
  • Your device + OS (e.g., iPhone 14 iOS 17.5, Pixel 7 Android 14)
  • App version (found in Profile → About)
  • Exact steps to reproduce the issue
  • What you expected vs. what happened
  • Screenshots or a short screen recording (if possible)
  • Time of occurrence + your timezone

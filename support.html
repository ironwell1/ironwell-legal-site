Support

How to contact us and report issues

Delete Your Account

In the Ironwell app, go to: Profile > Delete Account. This removes your account and associated training data.

Report a Bug

Please email support@ironwellapp.com and include:

  • A clear subject line, e.g., Bug: crash when adding new exercise
  • Your device and OS (e.g., iPhone 13 iOS 17.5, or Pixel 7 Android 14)
  • App version (Profile > About)
  • Exact steps to reproduce (step-by-step)
  • What you expected vs. what happened
  • Any screenshots or screen recordings (if possible)
  • Time of occurrence (with timezone)
